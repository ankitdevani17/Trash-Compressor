Problem :

As the tremendous increase in the population is observed , unclean and unhygienic environments have developed in our whole surrounding which  leads to overflow of waste in dustbins of many regions and polluted environment which turns into a hazard for lives. The management of these trash has also arised due to the extensive amount of trash produced by societies all over the world.

Solution Proposed :

To reduce these issues of huge amounts of production of trash, we could make a dustbin which compresses the trash as soon as it enters the dustbin.The Trash compressor is made of a scissor shaped jack made of wooden blocks and compression is done with the help of the wooden plank. The X compressor  enhances the trash holding capacity to a greater extent so people will not need to dispose of trash outside the dustbin and the environment will remain hygienic.

Future Scope :

  • The issue of improper trash disposal outside dustbins would drastically decrease as overfilling of dustbin won’t be an issue
  • The Sanitation workers would not have to work in unhygienic environments as the littering will be acutely reduced. Ergo, the investment for this product is doubly beneficial
  • Replacing the wooden X frame with a proper metal (eg: Aluminum) will lead to cost reduction in the long term.
  • The landfills would have the compressed trash which could increase the amount of trash dumping.

Limitations:

  • The product consumes some power so we need a rechargeable battery.
  • Usage of solar energy is also a good option but it comes with high cost for the system.
  • Wet waste would adversely affect the wooden plank and the X wooden plates .
  • Compression of heavy metals is not feasible.
  • Compared to the normal dustbins, there are some additional parts used e.g. motor, wood, battery, lead screw which makes the product heavier , more expensive and restricted movement..
  • The lifetime of the wooden frame would vary depending upon the usage, and hence will require changing if the use is rigorous.
