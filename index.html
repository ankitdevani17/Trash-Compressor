Trash Compressor

Irresponsible disposal of trash leads to unhealthy living conditions and surroundings.
 In addition, it creates an unsafe workplace condition for the collection workers.â€‹ 

Idea of the product  

One of the ways to reduce the quantity of the trash collected could be done by preparation of a  dustbin which compresses the trash after a sufficient amount of compressible trash enters the dustbin through the opening provided at the side of the dustbin. This enhances the trash holding capacity to a greater extent so people will not need to dispose of trash outside the dustbin and the environment will remain hygienic. 

An X-compressor will be made which will be attached inside the dustbin. The X compression will make use of Pull-push and Rotation Motion techniques.
